# Fini — Google Play Store Listing

## App name (30 chars max)

Fini — Finish What You Start

## Short description (80 chars max)

One quest at a time. No todo lists. No pile-up. Local-first and private.

## Full description (4000 chars max)

Finish what you start.

Fini replaces the task list with a quest log. Instead of a wall of things to do, you see one active quest at a time — your current mission, not everything you've ever meant to finish.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
HOW FINI WORKS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

• One quest at a time. The app leads, you execute. No planning, no sorting, no prioritizing.

• Spaces for context. Group quests by area of life — Personal, Work, a side project. Every quest belongs to exactly one Space.

• Energy-aware. Tell Fini how you feel today. Low energy surfaces lighter quests. High energy unlocks bigger chunks.

• Abandon without guilt. Quests you no longer want can be closed without shame. Completed and abandoned quests live in History — out of sight, still recoverable.

• Zero pile-up. The app never shows you a heap of unfinished work. There is no backlog anxiety.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
PRIVACY BY DESIGN
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Everything runs on your device. No accounts. No cloud sync required. No analytics, no telemetry, no tracking.

Your quests are yours. They never leave your phone unless you explicitly choose to share them.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
OPTIONAL PEER SYNC
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Want to sync a Space with a partner, a co-founder, or a trusted friend? Fini supports peer-to-peer sync over your local network — but only between devices you explicitly approve. No server in the middle, no account required on either side. You stay in control of who sees what.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
WHO IT'S FOR
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

• People who have tried many productivity apps and abandoned each within a month.
• People who want to finish things, not curate lists.
• People who believe their productivity data should not be a dataset.

Fini is in active development. New features ship regularly. Early users shape the direction.
